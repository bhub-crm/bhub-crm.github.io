Jack is the owner of the local pharmacy. He recently found a new supplier who is offering lower pain killers prices so he gradually started placing his orders with him.

Jessica got a notification on her app that Jack is forecasted to leave. Time for her to take action!​

After negotiating the price, Jessica sets up a Customer Individual Price for Jack. Based on his sales history and the smart article recommendations, Jessica offers some overstock medicine, which complements Jack's regular purchases.

Jessica is now happy to have managed to keep Jack as a customer.
