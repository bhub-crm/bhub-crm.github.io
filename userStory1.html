Matt recently signed up with his new paint supplier. He is planning going there within the next week to make his first big bulk purchase.

Via the Hub Dashboard, Matt was assigned to Jessica’s territory right away by her Sales Manager and her Hub app notified her about her new customer which hasn’t been visited yet.

Navigating to her daily planner and having an overview of her entire day she find a free spot and immediately sets up an appointment to visit Matt.​

Due to Jessica’s quick actions Matt doesn’t need to go to the store and she has all the insights she needs to give him the most suitable products and even place orders for him.

One month later, Jessica gets a notification that it is once again time to visit matt. Thanks to the customer insights, she can now see all their activities history and documented notes so she is prepared for the meeting. ​ On the Hub Dashboard her manager can keep track on her activities and is happy that she is hitting her targets.
