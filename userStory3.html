Jessica, a sales rep for a big beverage shop is looking for new customers in her territory to grow and assign to her portfolio.

She gets in contact with Tim, a bar owner, who’s purchases have been slowly increasing in the past 2 months. Jessica fills out a “customer potential survey” and realizes he is purchasing his soft drinks from a competitor.

Marketing is currently running a campaign on soft drinks and biers which seem to be fitting for Tim​

By checking her article recommendations, she finds out about the campaign. Perfect opportunity for her to hook him up and convert his purchases to her.​

Tim is now fully stocked to make some delicious cocktails and Jessica makes sure to schedule her visits in the happy hour. ​
